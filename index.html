MY    STORY

There is something about creation that had always had me captivated. In my youth I had discovered the power of audio production and graphic design. The thrill of creating lively images or melodies that made your skin tingle was something that drove me down a path into the music industry.

At the age of 14, I had produced my first EP which lead me into a career of DJ'ing and producing. Under the alias of M Kraft, I signed to a UK based record label in 2007 and released a second EP which at the time had served me very well.

Simultaneously, I had started a record label with the main focus of helping Australian Rock Bands break the scene. Through Silverback Records, we were able to help many artists gain exposure and publish their music, it was an exhilarating experience.

I had partnered in a management company at the same time named Insomnia Management, through this we not only signed the bands, but managed them as well. From creating press releases, album art, event posters and websites, it lead me down a path that fueled my thirst for creativity.

In 2012 I decided to leave it all behind due to the music industry taking a hard hit from the availability of the internet and left for overseas. What was meant to be a 3 month holiday ended up in being a year and a half. I came back with my now wife and jumped straight into industries that were far from ideal. From BMW Car sales to Interior Fitouts, the social aspects were great but I felt a yearning for something creative.

Web developement quickly sprang to mind. However how much it had changed in 15 years was daunting. I took it upon myself to try and learn as much as I could in the shortest of times which has lead me to where I am now. I am no expert, I am no genius, but I find myself captivated like I had once been with my audio production. As of June 2021, I became a father which has only created a further drive to become a better developer. The world is at my fingertips, pun intended, and I aim to expand my skill base further.
